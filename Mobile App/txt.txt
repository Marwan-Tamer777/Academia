the steps to run the app: 
1- Download Android studio 
2- Download Flutter SDK 
3- Downnload Dart SDK 
4- open the project on Android studio 
5- from device manager create your emulator or use usb to run the app on your phone 
6- to run the app on your phone you must turn on the developer options by going to settings - about phone - version and click many times until the device show you a message "you are now a developer" 
then turn on usb debugging to enable the instalation 
7- finally when the emulator or your phone is connected to the project you will be able to run the app easily from run sign in Android studio
